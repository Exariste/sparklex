About Sparklex

Our Story

A home care brand built around one idea — every home deserves products that are effective, safe and genuinely affordable.

Our Purpose

Purity, in every product we make.

Sparklex Home Care's goal is to deliver effective, safe and high-quality home care products for every household in Bangladesh. From laundry to floors, dishes to glassware, every product is formulated to make daily cleaning simpler, safer and more dependable.

We believe that a clean home shouldn't come at the cost of safety or affordability — and that belief shapes every formula, every package and every interaction with the people who trust our products.

Mission

"Delivering Purity, Care and Excellence to Every Home."

Every product we release is held to this single standard — does it bring real purity, real care, and real excellence into someone's home?

Vision

To become one of Bangladesh's most trusted home care brands.

We're building Sparklex product by product, household by household — earning trust through consistency rather than claiming it upfront.
